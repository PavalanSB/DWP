Getting Started

Welcome to the Digital Wellness Platform! Here's how to make the most of it:

  • Log Activities: Go to the Activity page to record your daily screen time, sleep, and mood.
  • Set Goals: Use the Goals page to define targets for screen time, study, and sleep.
  • View Insights: Check the Dashboard and Charts for trends and ML-powered recommendations.
  • Customize: Update your profile and settings for a personalized experience.
